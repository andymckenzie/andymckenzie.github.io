Andrew McKenzie’s Home Page

Hello out there. Welcome to my academic website. I’m currently a psychiatry resident in the physician-scientist program at the Icahn School of Medicine at Mount Sinai, which is in NYC. My primary research interests are in Alzheimer’s disease and related disorders, brain banking and preservation, and computational biology. Thanks for visiting.
